It's no secret that society is not ideal. Violence and harmful crime is very frequent, examples being
murders, mass shootings, rape, severe physical and mental abuse, and acts of terrorism. It's true that
society tries to prevent this in different ways, from policing to justice and incarceration, to simply
trying to prevent bad things from happening in the first place. But what if all of that could be better?
This is what this article intends to turn its focus on--what can be improved, and how will it help to
create a better society than what we presently have? Look at me go!
