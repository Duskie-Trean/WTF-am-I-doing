Significance & Conclusion


This is an area that is not delved into by most studies, as most studies are examining the
bare ideas rather than their synthesis and broader application. Each of the individual ideas
are important as the success of any one (or sometimes a pair) of ideas, if functional, would
improve society in some part, however, if the batch of ideas could be successfully synthesized
into a coherent, functional system, it would have massive impact on the discussion of how
societies should or could be designed in the realm of political philosophy–especially as technology
develops and we learn new things–and ways in which we could improve our own to better protect
the many, many humans who have horrible things done to them by other humans. If there were to
be a way that a country could protect or otherwise aid their citizens, the many individuals,
without costing them their liberties, that would be incredibly beneficial to every nation which
values its people–in essence, the entire world.
